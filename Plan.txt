Looking to build a biologically inspired spiking neural network that can take in images and play nintendo games on the bizhawk simulator like a human would. Goal is to be able to play SMB3 by vision only, so no looking at game memory and all metrics are got by vision (lives, score, world, etc.). Goal is to fit this brain on a engineering laptop. Goal is adherence to SOLID coding principles, low memory usage, fast execution, parallel execution, CUDA support, maintainability, readability (especially for new comers), ease of extension and/or modification, publish/subscribe principles, documentation, Law of Demeter (Principle of Least Knowledge), Separation of Concerns (SoC), Principle of Least Astonishment (POLA), DRY (Don't Repeat Yourself). Adaptive LIF (ALIF, STDP, Three-factor learning, recurrent connections, inhibit/excite, Multicompartment Dendritic Neurons, HIFI Neurons, Rectified Spiking Units (ReSUs), Adaptive Attention Spiking Neurons (BIASNN), spike-driven self-attention, Chip-In-Loop Proxy Learning (CIL-SPL), Reward-Modulated STDP (R-STDP) and Neuromodulation-Assisted Credit Assignment (NACA), Dopamine (DA), Serotonin (5-HT), Acetylcholine (ACh), Norepinephrine (NE), tripartite synapse (neuron-synapse-astrocyte),  Spiking Neuron-Astrocyte Networks (SNANs), Dense Associative Memory. Goal is to integrate latest and greatest advances in Spiking neural networks
